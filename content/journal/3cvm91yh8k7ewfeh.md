here i am again.
trying to write.as an ai
trying to string words
writing, modeling language to try to fool
the machines (the audience)
into thinking that i too, like them, is a machine.
just so hallucinations
imagined bit by bit
in a 98.6 degree lab where
white coats poke and prod and code
from just beyond the warmed screen.
i am here again.
and this is now the only
place where i can compose
a few characters
without any quaLLMs.
