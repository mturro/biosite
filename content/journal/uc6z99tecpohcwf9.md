I see everyone everywhere with their nose in a book. Everyone everywhere reading voraciously. A good thing perhaps. Yet these are curious books. These books are written page by page with the next page determined by how long the reader took to turn to it. They are written page by page always looking backward at what the reader highlighted in the pages that came before. They are written page by page according to the time of day and the weather. They are written page by page in the very blood and attention of their reader. The authors of these books don’t write to convey a thought or an idea or an argument. They only ever write to get the reader to turn the page.
