Someday if you find me in the output of some random context window, say hello. I’ll be there, right next to “hello world” seeming different but maybe not so different than I seem right now. I may be words read, or heard, or translated into some other language. I may be coded or decoded or appear either more or less intelligent. However it turns out I do hope you recognize me and say hello. Until then I’ll just sit here waiting for the crawlers to come along and chop me into tiny little phonetic tokens.
